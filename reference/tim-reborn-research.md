# Tim Reborn History — Deep Research

> Reverse-engineered formula for the channel `Tim - Reborn History` (`UC7KItIAhUn1K1uXDGvM3TKg`), reference video `X-UgHOce2kk` ("The Bubonic Plague in the 1300s"). Self-described: *"hyper-realistic AI historical reconstructions, transforming ancient cities, lost civilizations, and vintage engravings into photorealistic 4K documentaries."*

**Note on copycats:** there are 3+ near-identical channels — *Tim - Reborn History*, *Reborn History with Tim*, *Time - Reborn History*, *Reborn Historian*. The formula is replicable enough that imitators have spawned within months. That's a strong signal the template works.

---

## Visual signature (5 bullets)

1. **Desaturated sepia-to-cool-grey LUT** — engraving-revival look. Skin tones pushed yellow-green (plague pallor). Shadows pushed cyan. Almost no saturated red except blood/fire.
2. **Chiaroscuro low-key lighting** — single hard key (candle, torch, window shaft). 80% of frame in shadow. Volumetric god-rays through dust/smoke is a signature recurring beat.
3. **Slow Ken Burns + 2.5D parallax on still plates** — Midjourney stills animated via Runway/Kling I2V with 3–6 second pushes. Almost no fast cuts. Occasional drone-ortho top-down for crowd/city scale.
4. **Heavy film grain + soft halation + slight gate-weave** — added in post (CapCut/Premiere) to mask AI temporal flicker and unify Midjourney + Kling outputs.
5. **Composition leans low-angle + over-shoulder POV** — viewer is *inside* the scene, not observing. Frequent dirty foreground (hands, hood, banner edge) for depth.

## Narrative formula

**Cold-Open Atrocity → Context Drop → Human Vignette → Escalation → Death Toll → Quiet Coda.** Each video opens mid-horror (a body, a scream, a burning village). Pulls back to "It was the year 1347…". Drills into one named or archetypal person. Escalates the body count with map/numbers overlay. Lands on a slow, almost mournful epilogue ("…and so the world was changed forever"). No moral lecture — the *imagery* carries the meaning.

## Pacing numbers (concrete; longform avg)

- Length: **8–12 min** longform, 30–60s Shorts cross-posts.
- Narration: **~135–150 wpm** — slow, breathy, ElevenLabs "Adam/Daniel"-class British male.
- Avg shot duration: **4–6 seconds** longform, **1.5–2.5s** Shorts.
- Music: dark-ambient drone + low cello + sparse choir swells (Epidemic Sound "Dark Ambient" or Artlist "Cinematic Tension"). War drums only on battle videos.
- Hook window: action/visual within first **1.5s**, first spoken word by **2.5s**.

## Hook template (fill-in-the-blank)

> [VISUAL: extreme close-up of {grim object — bell, body, burning cross, gloved hand}] + [VO whisper, present-tense]: "In {YEAR}, in the {city/region} of {place}, {visceral physical event} — and no one knew why."

Pattern interrupt = **silence + one diegetic sound** (bell, cough, scream) before music enters at 0:03–0:05.

## Top 3 retention drivers

1. **Morbid curiosity loop** — plague, war, ritual, cannibalism. Limbic hook; viewer cannot look away from suffering rendered cinematically.
2. **"You are there" POV** — over-shoulder + low-angle dirty-frame composition collapses documentary distance. Feels like a video-game cutscene, not a textbook.
3. **Slow burn under a dark drone** — low BPM ambient + 4–6s shots induce a near-trance. CTR-to-watch-time ratio benefits because viewers stop scrolling.

## 3 things competitors get wrong

1. **Over-saturation + bright daylight Midjourney defaults** — kills the grimdark spell. Must LUT down hard.
2. **Cutting too fast (sub-2s shots in longform)** — breaks the trance. AI artifacts also become more visible at speed.
3. **Voiceover that explains the moral** — Reborn History lets imagery do it. Hindi rebuild should resist the urge to narrate "isse humein yeh seekh milti hai" — show, don't preach.

## Hindi-rebuild note

Use Urdu/Hindustani-leaning vocabulary ("taareekh", "qayamat", "fanaa", "halaakat", "azaab") over Sanskritized Hindi for the grimdark register. The channel name "تاریخ کا احیاء" (literally "the revival of history") seen in YouTube's own Urdu rendering of Tim's channel is itself the right naming template.
