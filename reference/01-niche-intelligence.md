# Agent 1 — Niche Intelligence

> Defines how the **AI historical reconstruction (grimdark longform)** niche behaves.

**INPUT:**
- NICHE: AI historical reconstruction — pandemics, fallen cities, "last day before disaster," daily life in dark eras. Long-form English faceless YouTube. Reference: Tim - Reborn History.

---

## [CONTENT TYPE]

Visual + narrative hybrid. Visual carries 70% of meaning; narrative provides era-anchor + body-count + named-individual hooks. Informational density secondary — viewer is here for atmosphere, not a textbook.

## [HOOK TYPE]

**Curiosity + emotional dread.** Cold-open with single grim image (body, burning village, plague cart) + a date stamp. No question hook ("did you know"), no shock-cut. The dread is the hook.

## [PACING MODEL]

**Slow.** 4–6 sec per shot longform. 12–25s holds on hero stills. ASMR-tier. Viewer is being lulled into a sleep-listen trance, not entertained.

## [RETENTION DRIVER]

Three layered drivers:

1. **Morbid curiosity loop** — limbic hook from rendered suffering (plague, war, ritual, atrocity). Viewer cannot look away from cinematic-grade horror.
2. **"You are there" POV** — over-shoulder + low-angle dirty-frame composition collapses the documentary distance. Feels like a video-game cutscene, not a textbook.
3. **Slow burn under dark drone** — low-BPM ambient + 4–6s shots induce a near-trance. Watch-time benefits because viewers stop scrolling and the algorithm rewards.

## [STRUCTURE TYPE]

**Fixed.** 6-stage arc (Cold Dread → Origin → Spread → Collapse → Aftermath → Echo) with hard percentage targets per stage. Variant axes operate within the fixed structure, not against it.

## [CORE FAILURE CONDITIONS]

What kills performance in this niche:

1. **Saturated colors / golden-hour Midjourney defaults** — kills the grimdark spell. Channel signature is a desaturated sepia-to-cool-grey LUT; bright daylight defaults instantly read as "AI slop history channel."
2. **Cuts faster than 4 seconds in longform** — breaks the trance. AI artifacts (extra fingers, melted faces) also become visible at speed.
3. **Voiceover that explains the moral** — Reborn-tier channels let imagery do it. Narration that tells viewer "this teaches us…" or "this reminds us…" breaks register.
4. **Anachronism in costume / object / language** — plague-doctor beak mask in 1347 (mask is 1619 Lorme design), eyeglasses pre-1286, printed books pre-1440, "Yersinia pestis" in narrator's mouth (named 1894). One anachronism = comments full of "this isn't accurate" = view-through tank.
5. **Music that enters too early or too loud** — first 8 sec must be silent. Music must sit −20 LUFS+ under VO. Viewer is sleep-listening; loud music wakes them up and they bounce.
6. **Direct address to viewer** ("guys", "let me tell you", "now imagine") — kills ASMR spell. Reborn register is third-person mournful past-tense.
7. **Tonal break** — joke, modern reference, cheerful music. Genre cannot survive a single comedic interjection.
8. **Character drift** — recurring witness/narrator-character whose face changes between scenes. Viewer's brain registers it subconsciously and the spell breaks.
9. **Fast-cut Shorts grafted onto longform editing** — Shorts pacing (1.5–2.5s) used in longform reads as low-effort. Kills 8–18 min retention.
10. **Title sensationalism without payoff** — "INSANE" / "CRAZY" / "YOU WON'T BELIEVE" titles get clicks but bounce; this niche rewards literary titles ("The Bubonic Plague in the 1300s", "London Before the Fire") that match the calm grimdark register.
