## Notes

- Era cap: Roman costume (tunica + toga + paenula cloak), wattle-and-daub + tufa-stone Pompeian architecture, no Christian iconography (this is 79 CE; Christianity is 30+ years old but invisible in Pompeii).
- Anachronism trap: do NOT show medieval-style hooded plague-doctor figures. Roman physicians wore tunicae and used bronze instruments.
- Cultural beat: Pliny the Younger's letters to Tacitus (Letter 6.16, 6.20) are the canonical primary source — quote them.
- Visual motif: pick "burning city / smoke" + "ship arrival" (Pliny the Elder's rescue fleet) from the 4-axis menu.
- POV: Scholar-omniscient with letter-frame device (Pliny narrating retrospectively).
