## Notes

- Era cap: no gunpowder cannon (still ~80 years off in Islamic world for siege use); siege weapons here are mangonels + counterweight trebuchets.
- Anachronism trap: do NOT show the Mongols with Yuan-era armor (post-1271). 1258 Mongol kit is lamellar leather + iron plates, fur-lined.
- Cultural beat: House of Wisdom destruction → Tigris-runs-black-with-ink line is the signature image of this event.
- Visual motif: pick "burning city / smoke" from the 4-axis menu.
- POV: Scholar-omniscient (Juvayni was actually present, recorded the event).
