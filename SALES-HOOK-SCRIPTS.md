## 🎙️ Production notes (all variants)

**Voice:**
- ElevenLabs "Adam" (English, calm-builder tone) OR self-record (faster + more trust)
- Pace: 165–185 wpm (Short density — slightly faster than docs)
- No reverb. Dry. Direct.

**Music:**
- Lo-fi tech beat OR sub-bass cinematic — 90–105 BPM
- Level: −16 dB under VO
- Source: Suno v4 ("lofi tech, 95 bpm, sub-bass, focused"), Epidemic, Artlist

**SFX:**
- Hook: subtle whoosh on first cut
- Each scene cut: soft tick / click
- CTA: rising synth note + "ding" on comment text appearing

**On-screen text style:**
- Bold sans-serif (Inter / Helvetica Black)
- Yellow on dark grey OR white on near-black
- Animate on with subtle scale-up (1.0 → 1.05 over 0.2s)
- Always lower-third placement (don't block face if talking head)

**Cut rhythm:**
- 1.5–2.5s per cut average
- Hook scene = 2 micro-cuts to immediately establish motion
- CTA scene = single hold on product cover for 2s

**Aspect:** 9:16 vertical, 1080×1920. Export H.264, 30 fps, 16 Mbps.
